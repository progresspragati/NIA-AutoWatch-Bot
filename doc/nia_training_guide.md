# National Insurance Academy (NIA) - Online Training Portal Documentation

This document provides a guide to the NIA Pune's Online Training Portal for Insurance Brokers, covering registration, course requirements, and progress monitoring.

---

## 🏛️ About the Portal

The **National Insurance Academy (NIA)** training portal is a specialized video-based platform designed for **Insurance Brokers**. It facilitates mandatory training for both new entrants (Freshers) and existing professionals (Renewal).

- **Official Website**: [onlinetraining.niapune.org.in](https://onlinetraining.niapune.org.in/index.php)
- **Primary Focus**: Certification for Insurance Brokerage courses.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started: Registration

The portal offers two types of registration paths:

### 1. Fresher Course Registration

- **Target Audience**: Individuals seeking to start a career as an Insurance Broker.
- **Mandatory Training**: Typically requires completing **50 hours** of video content.
- **Validity**: 60 days from the date of payment.

### 2. Renewal Course Registration

- **Target Audience**: Existing Brokers renewing their license.
- **Mandatory Training**: Typically requires completing **25 hours** of video content.
- **Additional Requisites**: Must upload a "Brokers' Exam Passing Certificate" and "Renewal Certificate."

---

## 📝 Registration Guidelines & Requirements

To ensure a smooth registration, please adhere to the following technical requirements:

| Requirement      | Specification                                                            |
| :--------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Photograph**   | JPEG format, file size **under 30 KB**.                                  |
| **Photo Naming** | `PANNumber_FullName.jpeg` (e.g., `ABCDE1234F_JohnDoe.jpeg`).             |
| **Certificates** | PDF format, file size **under 100 KB** (Renewal only).                   |
| **Security**     | Requires valid PAN, Email, and Aadhaar number.                           |
| **Verification** | Email verification via OTP is required before filling the detailed form. |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Course Credentials**: Upon successful payment, your login credentials will be sent to your registered email. Check your spam/junk folder if not received in your inbox.

---

## 🎓 Course Completion & Certification

The training is strictly monitored based on "Active Video Watching" hours.

### Technical Requirements for Certification

1.  **Total Hours**: You must complete the required hours (**50 for Fresher / 25 for Renewal**).
2.  **Minimum Duration**: The certificate is only generated after **at least 5 days** from the start of training.
3.  **Active Monitoring**: The system tracks the exact amount of time spent on each video.

### Tracking Your Progress

You can monitor your progress through the **Reports** section:

- **Course Completion Report**: Check your overall "Completed" status.
- **User Summary**: View the time spent on each specific video.
- **Course Completion Log**: View the total cumulative hours spent on the platform.
